About Green Fresh Harvests

Your trusted partner for premium fresh fruits in Uganda

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Kyengera, Kampala, Green Fresh Harvests began as a small family business with a simple mission: to bring the freshest, highest-quality fruits directly from local farms to your table.

What started as a passion for supporting local farmers and providing healthy, nutritious options to our community has grown into Uganda's trusted source for premium fresh fruits.

Today, we work directly with over 50 local farmers across Uganda, ensuring fair prices for growers while delivering exceptional quality to our customers.

Our Mission & Values

Sustainability

We promote sustainable farming practices that protect our environment while producing the best fruits.

Fair Trade

We ensure fair compensation for our partner farmers, supporting local communities and families.

Quality First

Every fruit is carefully selected and inspected to meet our high standards before reaching you.

Community Focus

We're committed to strengthening our local community through employment and economic growth.
